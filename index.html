About Us


Path Care & Research Centre



Welcome to Path Care, a beacon of precision and empathy in the realm of diagnostics. Established in 2024, our laboratory has undertaken a transformative journey, driven by a profound commitment to redefine the standards of healthcare. Our story is one of innovation, compassion, and an unwavering pursuit of excellence.
At the heart of Path Care is a dedicated team of professionals who share a common goal – to provide accurate and timely results that empower individuals on their health journey. We understand that each diagnosis holds profound significance, influencing treatment decisions and ultimately shaping the quality of life for our patients.Read more
Our state of art facility is a testament to our commitment to cutting-edge technology. From advanced diagnostic equipment to sophisticated testing methodologies, we leave no stone unturned in ensuring the precision of our results. This commitment to innovation is not merely about staying ahead in the field but, more importantly, about providing our patients with the best possible care.
The year 2024 marked the genesis of our journey, and since then, Path Care has evolved into a trusted name in the realm of diagnostics. Our growth is not just measured in terms of infrastructure and technology but is deeply rooted in the relationships we build with our patients and the communities we serve.
Our mission extends beyond the confines of a laboratory. It is a commitment to being a reliable partner in health, offering not just accurate results but also a supportive environment for individuals navigating the complexities of their well-being. Path Care envisions a future where healthcare is not just a service but a collaborative journey towards a healthier society.
In every test we conduct, we see an opportunity to make a positive impact. Path Care is not just a diagnostic center; it is a testament to our dedication to transforming healthcare, one precise result at a time. Join us on this journey where precision meets compassion, shaping a healthier and more informed future for all.
